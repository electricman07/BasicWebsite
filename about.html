About

Adrian Popowich

Production Designer

Adrian Popowich is a production designer with a passion for creating immersive environments that enhance storytelling. With a background in both art and architecture, Adrian brings a unique perspective to every project.

His work has been featured in various film and television productions, showcasing his ability to blend creativity with practicality. Adrian is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of design while maintaining a strong focus on collaboration and communication with directors and crew.

When not on set, Adrian enjoys exploring new design trends, traveling, and sharing his insights through his blog.
